This is not just a snake game - this is Arcade Snake!
The game consists of 10 levels. Collect points, make your snake bigger and bigger in order to finish all the levels.
Every level has required amount of points to collect.
When you will reach the point number required to finish the level you will go to the next one automatically.
Avoid barriers and your own body to pass through all the levels!
Controls:
W-Move Up, S-Move Down, A-Move Left, D-Move Right or arrow keys anallogically.
I hope you will enjoy the game.
Thank you for playing and have a nice day!
